Privacy Policy

Solvaniq · Rahmati Dev Studio
Effective date: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Solvaniq ("the app"), developed and published by Rahmati Dev Studio, based in the United Kingdom, handles your information. Solvaniq is a flashcard and spaced repetition learning app.

In short: your study data is stored locally on your device. We do not run our own servers, so we never receive or store your cards. We do not sell your data, and there are no ads. Optional features use Google services, including Google Sign-In, Google Drive backup, Firebase, and Google Play Billing, which process limited information as described below.

1. Information stored on your device

Everything you create in Solvaniq, including boxes, flashcards, images, audio, spelling words, review progress, statistics, and app settings, is stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device unless you choose to export it or back it up to Google Drive.

2. Google Sign-In (optional)

Solvaniq offers an optional Google Drive backup feature. If you choose to use it, you sign in with your Google account. During sign-in, the app receives:

  • Your email address – shown inside the app so you can see which account you are signed in to.
  • A sign-in identifier – provided by Google to associate the sign-in with your Google account.

Solvaniq requests only the minimum permissions it needs: access to its own private backup folder in your Drive, your email address, and standard sign-in. It does not request your name, profile photo, contacts, calendar, or any other Google data.

We do not store this information on any server of ours, and we do not use it for advertising or marketing. If you never sign in, the app works fully without a Google account.

3. Google Drive backups

When you create a backup, the app uploads a copy of your study data to the application data folder of your own Google Drive. This is a hidden, app-private area of your Drive. Because of the limited permission the app requests (the drive.appdata scope), Solvaniq:

  • can only see, create, and delete its own backup files;
  • cannot read, change, or delete any of your other Google Drive files, documents, or photos.

The app keeps your 8 most recent backups and automatically removes older ones. You can delete individual backups inside the app at any time. You can also remove Solvaniq's access to your Google account at Google Account → Security → Your connections to third-party apps and services (myaccount.google.com/connections). See section 9 for full instructions on deleting your backups.

4. Google and device services used by the app

Solvaniq uses Google Play services and Firebase configuration to enable Google Sign-In and the Google Drive backup feature. The app does not use analytics services and does not show advertisements.

Firebase may generate a Firebase Installation ID for an installed instance of Solvaniq, together with limited technical information needed to operate and maintain Firebase services, such as your device model, operating system version, and the versions of the Firebase components included in the app. This identifier relates to an app installation and is not intended to directly identify an individual. Rahmati Dev Studio does not use it for advertising, user profiling, or analytics.

Google processes sign-in, Drive, and Firebase requests under its own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Solvaniq can read your flashcards and spelling words aloud using the text-to-speech engine built into your device. When you use a read-aloud feature, the text on the card or the spelling word is passed to that engine, which is provided by your device or by a text-to-speech app you have installed. Depending on which engine you use and its settings, it may process the text on your device or over the internet. Solvaniq does not control this and does not receive anything back other than the spoken audio. If you would rather no text was passed to the engine, do not use the read-aloud features.

5. Subscriptions and payments

Solvaniq offers optional Premium subscriptions purchased through Google Play. All payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment card details. Purchases are linked to your Google Play account and can be managed or cancelled in the Google Play Store.

Google Play processes your payment information, transaction records, subscription status, renewals, cancellations, and refunds under its own terms and privacy policy. The app receives purchase status and related product information from Google Play as necessary to confirm access to Premium features. Rahmati Dev Studio does not receive or store your payment card details.

6. Notifications

Solvaniq can show optional local reminders, such as daily review reminders. These are created by the app on your device and are never sent from any server or over the internet. You can turn them off in the app settings or in your device's system settings at any time.

7. Import, export and folder backups

You can export your boxes as files and import files into the app. Export files are created by the app directly on your device and are never uploaded to any server. These actions use your device's file picker and only access the files you select. Exported files are saved where you choose and are under your control.

Solvaniq also offers an optional folder backup feature. If you enable it, the app writes backup files to a folder on your device that you pick yourself, either manually or automatically when the app opens and closes. These backups never leave your device unless you move them. You can choose how many backup files the app keeps: only the newest one, the newest 8, or all of them. Older files beyond your chosen limit are removed automatically from that folder.

8. Children's privacy

Solvaniq is a general audience study tool and is not directed at children under 13. The app does not require an account, and Rahmati Dev Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Your study data stays on your device and is never sent to us. If you choose to use the optional Google features described in this policy, Google may process limited information under its own privacy policy.

9. Data retention and deletion

Rahmati Dev Studio does not run any servers and does not hold a copy of your Solvaniq data. There is therefore nothing for us to retain, and no additional retention period applies. You are always in direct control of your own data, and you can delete all of it yourself at any time using the steps below.

How to delete your data

  • Data on your device (boxes, flashcards, images, audio, spelling words, progress, statistics, and settings): delete individual items inside the app, or uninstall Solvaniq to remove the app's locally stored data from your device. Copies may still exist in files you exported yourself, in folder backups, or in Google Drive backups, so check the other items below if you want to remove every copy.
  • Google Drive backups: open Solvaniq, go to the Google Drive Sync screen, and delete your backups there. This is the reliable way to remove them, so do it before the steps below. To then revoke Solvaniq's access to your Google Account, go to Google Account → Security → Your connections to third-party apps and services (myaccount.google.com/connections), select Solvaniq, and choose Remove access. Note that removing access stops Solvaniq from reaching your Google Account, but it may not delete backups that are already there. If you have already removed access or uninstalled the app, you can still delete the backups from Drive itself: go to Google Drive → Settings → Manage apps, find Solvaniq, and choose Delete hidden app data.
  • Folder backups: these are ordinary files saved in the folder you chose on your device. To delete them, open that folder with your device's file manager and delete the files yourself. The app cannot do this for you.

What is deleted and what is kept

  • Deleted: the data on your device, and the Google Drive and folder backups, once you carry out the steps above. Because we hold no copy, no version of it remains with us. Copies that you exported, moved, or stored somewhere else stay under your control and must be deleted separately.
  • Kept by Google, not by us: your purchase records are held by Google Play, and technical information such as the Firebase installation identifier described in section 4 is held by Google. These are covered by Google's privacy policy and are outside our control.

If you have any questions about deleting your data, or you would like us to confirm that we hold nothing about you, email us at rahmati.dev.studio@gmail.com and we will reply within 30 days.

10. Security

Your data is stored using your device's standard app storage protections. Communication with Google services, such as sign-in and Drive backups, is handled by official Google libraries over encrypted connections.

Backup files created by Solvaniq are ZIP archives. They are transferred over encrypted connections, but Solvaniq does not separately password-protect or end-to-end encrypt them. Anyone with access to your device, your chosen backup folder, or your Google account may be able to open them, so keep your device and Google account secure.

Solvaniq disables Android cloud backup for its app data, so your study data is not copied to your Google account by the platform. However, on some Android devices, the operating system or device manufacturer may still support direct device-to-device migration. Please do not rely on that, and create a Solvaniq backup before reinstalling the app or changing devices.
